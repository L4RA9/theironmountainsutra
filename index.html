The Iron Mountain Sutra

Why am I bound to the floor? Thus the wind followed the Buddha's death
around The Iron Mountain.

Along the variable raiment are the altitudes
and the wind searching for the sea-mind bird
Searching for the bird's feet near the ellipsis.
The wind searches for the abundant shore
of sweet mulberries near the ellipsis. The wind is searching
on the peak in a particle of tranquilizing fire
