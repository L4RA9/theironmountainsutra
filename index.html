The Buddha walked on altitudes
along variable raiment. Each moment a stair
is placed for the simple clothing
made of atmosphere
floating up from the no-mind
Born from the cyclone
from the ellipsis
from the native body
of sweet mulberries
At the peak, snow and Samsara appear
in a particle of tranquilizing fire
following the Buddha's death
around The Iron Mountain
