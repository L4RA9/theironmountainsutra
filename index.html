The Iron Mountain Sutra

Why am I bound to the floor? Thus the wind followed the Buddha's death
around The Iron Mountain.

Along the variable raiment are the altitudes



Wind searching for the sea-mind bird

searching for the bird's feet

near the ellipsis

searching for the abundant shore of sweet mulberries

near the ellipsis

The wind is searching

for the well

for the thirst

in a particle of tranquilizing fire
